Four federal pricing databases, one audit

Real rates from CMS.gov — not estimates, not averages.

PFS

Physician Fee Schedule

1,000,000+ procedure rates for office visits, surgeries, imaging, and evaluations. The backbone of outpatient billing.

CL

Clinical Lab Fee Schedule

2,130 lab test rates — blood panels, cultures, urinalysis, and diagnostics. Compare exactly what Medicare pays for labwork.

ASP

Average Sales Price (Drugs)

876 drug pricing rates. See the real per-unit cost for injectable and infused medications billed under Part B.

OP

Outpatient Prospective

18,986 APC rates for hospital outpatient services, facility fees, and ambulatory procedures.

Geographic Rate Matching

Rates adjusted by MAC locality for accurate regional comparisons. GPCI-aware matching for physician services.

Charity Care Checker

Identifies if your bill may qualify for hospital financial assistance or charity care programs based on billing patterns.
